About the Story

This is a soft release of a dystopian sci-fi novel, offered free online to gather audience feedback. There’s no publisher (Yet...), no ads, and no paywall. Just the story and hopefully some reactions.

You’re early, although this novel is complete, things may change. Chapters may be rewritten. Timelines might fracture. If you find plot holes, broken tech, or characters you love (or hate), your messages will be appreciated in any fashion.
