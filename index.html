About the Story

This is an experimental release of a dark sci-fi novel, offered free online to gather real reader feedback. There’s no publisher, no ads, and no paywall—just the story and your reactions.

You’re early. Things may change. Chapters may be rewritten. Timelines might fracture. If you find plot holes, broken tech, or characters you love (or hate), your messages help shape the next version of this book.
